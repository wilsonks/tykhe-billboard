About Cammegh SS2 Wheel

The SlingShot2(SS2) Roulette Wheel TT (Table Top) optimises the unique design characteristics of the incomparable Slingshot 2 automatic roulette wheel. The Slingshot 2 TT's height and diameter enables installation as an alternative to a traditional manual wheel on a 'live' table.By means of a manual 'push button start', the Slingshot 2 TT removes the possibility of dealer collusion and increases the number of games per hour. It can also continue to be used as the random number generator for electronic betting terminals even when the 'live' table is closed.
